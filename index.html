Peppermint Hot Chocolate

Warm up your winter festivities with this indulgent Peppermint Hot Chocolate. This comforting drink combines rich cocoa with a hint of peppermint, creating a delicious and festive treat. Garnish with whipped cream and crushed candy canes for an extra touch of holiday magic. Share a cup with friends and family by the fireplace or as a sweet ending to your Christmas dinner.

Christmas Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

- Sugar cookie dough
- Royal icing (in various colors)
- Festive sprinkles

Instructions:

1. Roll out the sugar cookie dough and cut into Christmas shapes using cookie cutters.
2. Bake the cookies according to the recipe instructions.
3. Once cooled, decorate the cookies with royal icing and sprinkles.
4. Allow the icing to set before serving.

Enjoy!

Peppermint Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

- Hot chocolate mix or cocoa powder
- Peppermint extract
- Whipped cream
- Crushed candy canes

Instructions:

1. Prepare hot chocolate according to package instructions or using your favorite homemade recipe.
2. Stir in a few drops of peppermint extract to taste.
3. Pour the hot chocolate into mugs and top with a generous dollop of whipped cream.
4. Sprinkle crushed candy canes over the whipped cream.

Enjoy!
